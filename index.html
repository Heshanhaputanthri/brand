GDP per Capita

Which countries have the strongest Economies?


Gross domestic product (GDP) is the total monetary or market value of all the finished goods and services produced within a country over a certain time period. It is an indicator of the economies size and performance. GDP per Capita is the GDP per indivisual within the country. It is a useful metric to make cross-country comparisons of average living standards.

GDP per Capita strongly correlates with a number of metrics and indicaotrs which measure the general living standards of an economy such as life expectancy, happiness and freedom to make life choices.
